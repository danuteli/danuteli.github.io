Posters Matar

A poster and tote bag design for a fictional music event, inspired by the character and name of my cat — Matar.

The project explores the word's layered meanings in Arabic and Portuguese, reflects the mysterious, dual nature of Matar himself. The task involved using analogue printing techniques to produce the design in multiple pieces, then adapting it to a smaller scale for printing on tote bags.

Silkscreen print on paper and fabric.
Created for a university assignment in 2024.
